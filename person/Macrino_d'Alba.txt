#Name#
Macrino d'Alba

#Life Time#
1460-1510

#Introduction#
Macrino d'Alba (c. 1460–1465 – c. 1510–1520) was an Italian painter of the Renaissance period, active mainly in Piedmont. Also called Giovanni Giacomo Fava. Born in Alba, he painted the Resurrection for the chapel of Sant’Ugone at the Certosa di Pavia. He also painted in the church of the Franciscans at Alba and works in the Vigevano Cathedral.
