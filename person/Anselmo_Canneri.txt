#Name#
Anselmo Canneri

#Life Time#
1552-1575

#Introduction#
Anselmo Canerio (active 1552-1575) was an Italian painter of the Renaissance period. He was born in Verona. He worked with the young Paolo Veronese at the Villa Soranzo near Treviso in 1552,[1] and subsequently is recorded painting at Castelfranco, Vicenza, and Verona.
