#Name#
Cristoforo Ciocca

#Life Time#
1462-1542

#Introduction#
Cristoforo Ciocca (1462-1542) was an Italian painter of the late-Renaissance, active in Milan. Little biographical information is known, except that he was the pupil of Gian Paolo Lomazzo. He painted a San Cristoforo altarpiece for the church of San Vittore al Corpo, Milan . He mainly painted sacred subjects and portraits. [1][2]
