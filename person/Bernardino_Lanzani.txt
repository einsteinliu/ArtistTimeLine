#Name#
Bernardino Lanzani

#Life Time#
1460-1530

#Introduction#
Bernardino Lanzani (1460-c. 1530) was an Italian painter of the Renaissance, active mainly in Pavia and Bobbio.

He is also known as Bernardino Colombano since he was born in San Colombano al Lambro. He was a pupil of Ambrogio da Fossano. He painted a triptych for the church of Santa Maria del Carmine, Pavia. He painted for the Abbey of San Colombano in Bobbio, and the chruch of San Teodoro in Pavia.
