#Name#
Giovanni Battista Ramacciotti

#Life Time#
#Introduction#
Giovanni Battista Ramacciotti was an Italian painter of the Baroque period, active in Siena.

He was a priest of Siena, who lived about the middle of the 17th century, and practised portrait and historical painting. In the Franciscan church at Siena there is a Nativity by him, and at uffizi in Florence, a Birth of the Virgin. Abraham Bloemaert engraved after him the portrait of a Nun, Colomba da Tofaninis.
