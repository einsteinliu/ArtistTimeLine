#Introduction#
Giovanni Battista Tinti (late 16th century) was an Italian painter of the Renaissance period. He studied first under Orazio Samacchini in Bologna, and subsequently established himself in Parma, where he took Tibaldi, Correggio, and Parmigianino for his models. He painted an Assumption for the cathedral of Parma.
