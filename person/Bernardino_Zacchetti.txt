#Introduction#
Bernardino Zacchetti (active c. 1523) was an Italian painter of the Renaissance period. He was born in Reggio Emilia. His style recalls Raphael, and is also said to have worked with Michelangelo in the Sistine chapel. His picture of St. Paul in the church of San Prospero at Reggio recalls Il Garofalo.
